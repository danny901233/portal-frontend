🔧 Integration Architecture

Bridged SIP Forwarding - Infinity stays in the call path

📞 Customer calls Infinity tracking number
↓
🔀 Infinity routing: Hours + Available → Phone | No Answer → AI
↓
🌉 Bridged SIP forwarding to LiveKit (Infinity records entire call)
↓
🤖 ReceptionMate AI handles conversation
↓
📊 Summary sent to Infinity platform + Customer CRM
