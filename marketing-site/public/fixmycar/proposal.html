The problem

FixMyCar loses a large share of bookings to cancellations, no-shows and customers taken off-platform to dodge the fee. With around a third of bookings cancelling, that is thousands of lost commissions a month, and it repeats on every customer's next service. ReceptionMate is an AI layer that protects every booking from the moment it is made.

What ReceptionMate runs

1
Booked and confirmed. The AI confirms each booking and masks contact, so chat stays on-platform.
2
Garage relay. Pre-visit questions are handled through the AI, and numbers are never shared.
3
Remind and save. Friendly reminders go out, and anyone trying to cancel is offered a reschedule first.
4
After the job. A quick "did it go OK?" confirms attendance and earns a review.
5
Next time. The AI rebooks the next MOT or service and quotes last visit's advisories.

Delivered by WhatsApp, SMS and AI voice through ReceptionMate's existing platform. Once a car is dropped off, the garage and customer deal directly, and on-site or extra work stays out of scope.

You only pay the 3% on jobs that actually complete, so a cancelled booking costs you nothing. We are paid when, and only when, you earn your commission.

Our incentive is the same as yours. Because the fee is on completed jobs, every cancellation we save becomes another completed job, which means more commission for you and a slightly larger fee for us.

What we will prove in 3 months

You get weekly reporting throughout, and a full review at month three against your starting numbers:

  • Cancellation rate, down (fewer lost jobs).
  • Completion rate, up (more jobs that earn you commission).
  • Rebookings, returning customers won back on-platform.
  • No-shows, down, with post-job attendance confirmations.
  • Reviews, collected automatically after each job.

After 3 months

At the end of the trial we review the results together. If it is working, we move onto a full agreement. If it is not, you walk away, having only committed to three months.

What we need from FixMyCar

  • API access to bookings, so we can confirm, reschedule and rebook.
  • Customer opt-in to messaging, captured at booking.
  • Your commission figure (confidential), so we can apply the 3%.
  • A named point of contact for the three months.
